Jessica "Jessi" Rose Zawadowicz

Jessi Zawadowicz is an actor hailing from Baltimore, Maryland. With fifteen years of experience and a recent Bachelor of Arts degree from Baldwin Wallace University in Theatre with a minor in Film, Jessi plans to move to Los Angeles and take the Voice Acting world by storm.

This coming fall, Jessi will be participating in the Disney College Program at Walt Disney World Resorts in Orlando, Florida, working from August to January as a Character Attendant.
